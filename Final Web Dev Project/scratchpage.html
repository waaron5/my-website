Ben & Jerry's Peanut Butter World

This page is about my passion for ice cream. I enjoy trying new flavors and ranking my favorite kinds and ice cream stores.

My favorite ice creams right now

  1. Peanut Butter World
    • The mix of chocolatey chocolate ice cream and peanut butter ribbons with cookie swirls is untouchable
    • Great for peanut butter lovers and alone time
  2. Peanut Butter Half Baked (I like peanut butter)
    • This flavor is great because the ice cream base itself is peanut butter flavored
    • Goes well with friends and Survivor night on Paramount Plus
  3. Any vanilla ice cream WITH apple pie or peach cobbler OR a hot home-made brownie
    • The combination of vanilla ice cream with almost any hot fruit pie or warm chocolate brownie is immaculate
    • Good for family board game nights
